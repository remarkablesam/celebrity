Biography

Robyn Rihanna Fenty was born in Saint Michael, Barbados, and rose from Caribbean roots to become one of the most influential artists in modern pop and R&B. Her breakthrough came in the mid-2000s with chart-topping singles, followed by a run of albums that reshaped global music style and performance culture.

Beyond music, Rihanna expanded into beauty and fashion leadership with globally recognized brands, while using her platform to support humanitarian causes through large-scale education, emergency response, and climate-focused giving.
